FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the courses?
The duration of our courses varies from 3 months to 1 year, depending on the course and the level of expertise of the students.
What are the prerequisites for these courses?
We recommend that students have a basic understanding of programming concepts and languages. However, we also offer beginner-level courses for those who are new to programming.
What kind of job opportunities can I expect after completing these courses?
Our courses are designed to prepare students for careers in the industry. After completing our courses, students can expect to find job opportunities as web or app developers, database administrators, software engineers, and more.
What kind of support do you offer to students after completing the course?
We offer career counseling, job placement assistance, and ongoing support to our students after they complete the course. We also encourage our alumni to participate in our events and workshops to stay connected with the community.
Are there any scholarships or financial aid available for students?
Yes, we offer scholarships and financial aid to eligible students. Please contact our admissions office for more information.
Do you offer online courses?
Yes, we offer online courses for students who are unable to attend the classes in person. Our online courses are designed to offer the same quality of education and support as our in-person courses.
